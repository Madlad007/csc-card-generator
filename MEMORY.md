## Design Direction
- Mobile-first Streamlit interface focused on simple form entry, camera/photo capture, and one-click card generation.
- Final output is a portrait trading card at 1080x1350.
- Layout is adjustable through named coordinate constants for player image placement zones and text placement.
- Uses Pillow compositing around a static `assets/background.png` card template.
- Player cutout is background-removed with rembg, cleaned of remaining white fringe, tightly cropped to visible alpha bounds, enhanced, softly shadowed/glowed, and placed responsively inside a defined card zone.
- Player placement is subject-aware: full-body, half-body, and close-up uploads are classified by cropped aspect ratio and anchored differently for better composition.
- Optional foreground splash artwork can be layered over the player for integration with the poster/card artwork.
- Form values are drawn directly onto the final card image with uppercase player/team names and centered jersey/stat numbers.
- Dynamic text should place only entered values into blank areas, not over printed labels such as “Team Name,” “Jersey Number,” “Speed,” “Shooting,” or “Passing.”
